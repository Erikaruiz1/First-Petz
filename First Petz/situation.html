Common Situations:

These are some common situations that you will come across with all pets:

  • Sickness:

    When a pet gets sick, one of the first reactions is to start worrying. The first thing to do is calmly evaluate the sysmtoms that your pet is suffereing. Ensure that you pay well attentions to their needs and make sure that they are eating and drinking water. If your pet continues to have terrible symtoms, take them to the vet so that they can recieve a check-up. None of us want to see our pets suffer, so taking them to that vet would help save them.

  • Trainning:

    Training a pet is not the easiest thing in the world, there is a reason why people deticate their whole life to thsi career. We recommend taking it one step at a time as to not stress the pet and or yourself. If you see that no progress is being made, try using rewards like treats or toys to help. We also recommend reatching out to a local pet trainer to help train your pet professionally.

  • Pet Vs Pet

    If you see that your pets do not seem to be getting along, it is better not to force them together as that can cause harm to both of them.Let them gradually meet each other, so they know that they're not a threat to each other.
