FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

These are common questions that one may have about adopting a pet!

Q:Is it better to adopt or buy a pet?

A:In our opinion, it is better to adopt a pet rather than buy. When buying a pet, we are unsure if the seller is participating in illegal activities. Adopting a pet is saving them from the shelter homes, to give them a new life.

Q:How long should I wait to adopt a new born pet

A:It would depend on what type of animal you are thinking of adopting, it is recommended to wait until the pets are able to nurse on their own without the need of their birthmom, or a human haveing to bottle feed them.

Q: How old should my dog be to gets it's first vaccine?

A:Baby puppies are recommended to get their first vaccine after their first 4 months.

Q: How much will I be spending at the vet?

A:The price will depend on what exactly your reason for the visit is, here are some averages depending on state acording to CARECREDIT.COM

  • California: $71.45
  • Colorado:$77.72
  • Florida:$60.41
  • Alaska:$80.06
Q:Can my horse compete in races?

A: It depends on what type of horse you own, a proper racing horse is called a Thoroughbred.
