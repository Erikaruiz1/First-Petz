Welcome To First Petz!

First Petz is designed to help pet owners whether they are preparing for their first ever pet or are considering adopting a loveable pet.

What To Expect:

First Petz has the answer to all your common pet questions from what to prepare before even adopting your pet, to how to ensure that they remain destressed as they adapt to their new enviorement. We are aware of hwo stressful it is for both the pet owner and the pet to adapt to new changes, so we're here to make it just a bit more simple.

What We Focus On:

The First Petz team is devoted to ensuring that all pets can live there lives a proper memeber of the family! Adopting a pet is a big responsibility for anyone, so there are many steps needed to ensure that they are well taken care of and are able to live they're life for a long time. Pets are some of our most devoted friends, who will stick by your side no matter what, so it's best that we treat them well!

About Our Team

First Petz team members Jevaun Lyle and Erika Ruiz are set to ensure that all pets are treated as family and raised in a loving household with proper care! They hope that this information will be helpful in the preparations in welcoming a new family memmber! Welcome from the First Petz team!
