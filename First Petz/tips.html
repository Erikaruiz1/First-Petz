Tips

here are some tips to keep in mind when deciding on whitch pet you want to take home.

  • Make sure you have the space

    • Some pets take up more room then others, and you have to make sure you have the space in your home for them otherwise they may end up feeling cramped.

  • Keep in mind how active you are

    • Most pets need some for of activity to keep healthy, so make sure that you will be able to garentee that they will be able to achive that.

  • Learn your pets diet

    • Your pets diet is a massive contributer to their health, so make sure you know how they eat and what you need to do to keep them healthy.
