First Day Expectations:

What to expect on the first day of bringing your pet home:

The first day of bringing your pet home is one of the most stressfull, so here's what to expect:

  • Nerves:

    Very similar to the way that we react to a new enviorment,your pet will probably be nervous and overwhelmed as they are not aware of whats going on.

  • Ride Home:

    Accidents are bound to happen, especially if the pet is not used to a moving vehicle, if the pet is a pet that can go in the inside of the car, make sure to pring pee pads, food, water, and maybe a toy to distract them from the ride over.

  • Hiding

    Due to this being a new enviorment, your pet could decided to hide behind couches or anywhere they can find in order to avoide being seen. Ensure that you keep an eye on them to ensure that they don't hide anywhere that could hurt them. Also make sure that any doors are closed, as they might run outside to avoid due to their survival instinct.

  • Introductions:

    Ones you have ensured that your pet is confortable around you, it would be best to give them a tour of the house, especially the area in which they will sleep, play,etc. If you sense that they are prepared to meet others, introduce them to the family, but ensure to do it calmly as to not overwhelme them.

  • Emotions:

    On the first couple of days, your pet may seem sad and non-motivated to do anything. It is best to keep an eye on them, to ensure that they don't become ill, the reasons could be many things, like homesickness, nerves, seperation anxiety, etc.
